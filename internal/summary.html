Goals

Test bed for early OCI Service incubation
  • OCI Services are developed independently by OCI Engineering
  • Assemble a comprehensive use case
  • Validate end-user experience
Training tool on OCI Services
  • A-Team
  • Critical customers: hand-delivered; workshop can be customized
  • Field: Sales/Pre-Sales, Consulting, and Customer enablement
  • Public: Customers and Oracle developer community
Launchpad for time-to-market reference materials
  • MuShop can be quickly adapted to reflect changes in the platform, or showcase strategic OCI feature releases
  • Establish familiar and easily deployable applications/example suite to get started
Showcase OCI for Cloud Native workloads
  • Position Oracle within the enterprise cloud market
  • Modernized approach to enterprise application development
  • Choose Oracle over AWS, Google, Azure, IBM

Why Mu Shop?

MuShop is a polyglot microservices application purpose-built as a cloud native approach to OCI utilization
