What is MuShop?

  • An e-commerce reference app realized as a set of microservices developed on OCI
  • Real use case scenarios for OKE, ATP, Streaming, Networking, and more
  • Collection of self-paced, workshop-style exercises showcasing OCI features

Goals

  1. Test bed for early OCI Service incubation

    For execution purpose, services are developed independently by OCI Engineering. We put them together to ensure the parts fit as expected, and validate the end-user experience

  2. Training tool on OCI Services
    • A-Team
    • Critical customers: hand-delivered; workshop can be customized.
    • Field & General public: 100% self-service content
  3. Launchpad for expedited time-to-market reference materials

    Following the initial release, MuShop can be quickly adapted to reflect changes in the platform, or showcase strategic OCI feature releases

  4. Build credibility on Oracle‚Äôs support of micro-service Architecture
